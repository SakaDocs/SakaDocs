1.Sign in to your SakaDocs account or create one




2.On the home page, click on a document thumbnail or use the search bar at the top and choose document category. This will take you to a page with all the documents posted on that category.




3.To find your document, type in your names as they appear in your document on the input field on top of the documents. You can also scroll the documents until you find yours.




3.To claim your document, click on the claim this document button, just below your document



3.You will be taken to the payments page where you will get Mpesa payment instructions. Immediately after payment, you will receive an SMS with the mobile number of the Person that posted your document(the Poster) and a SAKADOCS code. Call the Poster and arrange to meet. The poster will give you your document in exchange for the SAKADOCS code you received via SMS. The poster will use the SAKADOCS code to claim his payment from SAKADOCS.
